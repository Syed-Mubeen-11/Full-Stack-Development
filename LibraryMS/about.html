About MyLibrary

Welcome to MyLibrary – your digital library management system. Our goal is to make books accessible anytime, anywhere.

Mission & Vision

  • Provide easy access to thousands of books digitally.
  • Encourage reading and learning for all ages.
  • Maintain up-to-date records for users and books.

Library Policies

  • Books can be issued for a period of 14 days.
  • Late returns may incur penalties.
  • Users are responsible for borrowed books.

Membership Guidelines

  • Registration is free for all users.
  • Users must login to issue and return books.
  • Maintain your account details accurately.
